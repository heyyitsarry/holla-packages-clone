**Design Movement:** Expedition Cartography — raw adventure meets editorial precision
**Core Principles:**
- Dark topographic textures as backgrounds, evoking maps and exploration
- Stark contrast between deep charcoal/navy and vivid lime-green accents
- Cards with slight tilt/rotation on hover for a tactile, physical feel
- Generous whitespace with intentional asymmetric column breaks

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) base with electric lime (#00E676) as the primary accent — evoking jungle trails and neon signage in South American cities. Secondary warm amber for price highlights.

**Layout Paradigm:** Masonry-style card grid with varying heights based on image aspect ratios. Filter bar is a horizontal sticky ribbon with pill toggles.

**Signature Elements:**
- Dotted/dashed route lines connecting destinations on card footers
- Subtle grain texture overlay on hero image
- Pill-shaped category chips with icon + label

**Interaction Philosophy:** Cards lift with a 3D perspective tilt on hover. Category chips toggle with a satisfying click animation.

**Animation:** Cards entrance with staggered fade-up. Filter chips spring-bounce on select. Hero text slides in from left.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for headings (condensed, bold, explorer feel) + "DM Sans" for body text. Prices in "Bebas Neue" with larger scale.

- Thick 2px borders on cards with square corners
- Oversized section labels ("PACKAGES") in condensed uppercase
- Terracotta price badges

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover reveals a subtle color wash over card images. Filter chips toggle with border color change.

**Animation:** Minimal — fade-in on scroll. No distracting motion.

**Typography System:** "Playfair Display" for hero title + "Barlow" for all other text. Clean editorial hierarchy.
